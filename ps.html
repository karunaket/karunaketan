PROJECT SINGULARITY

ROLE: Combat Designer ENGINE: Unreal Engine 5 GENRE: Action / TPS

01. THE DESIGN CHALLENGE

The Pivot: Originally envisioned as an open-world survival game, I pivoted early to a single-arena shooter to focus entirely on perfecting the "3Cs" (Character, Camera, Control).

The Goal: To engineer a "Visceral Feedback Loop" where every shot is communicated through Procedural Animation.

02. THE SOLUTION

The Mechanic: I built a custom Weapon Class in C++ that handles procedural recoil. I also implemented "Hit Stop" frames to emphasize impact.
