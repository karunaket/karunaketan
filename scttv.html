SHATTERED CHARMS

ROLE: Systems Designer ENGINE: Unreal Engine 5 GENRE: Turn-Based RPG

01. THE DESIGN CHALLENGE

The Problem: In many RPGs, dialogue choices feel disconnected from gameplay. Players can be rude to an NPC but still fight perfectly alongside them.

The Goal: Solve "Ludonarrative Dissonance" by making social choices impact combat stats.

02. THE SOLUTION

The Mechanic: I engineered a Data Table system where every dialogue choice carries a hidden "Affinity Value." High affinity unlocks "Synergy Buffs" (+20% Dmg) in combat.
